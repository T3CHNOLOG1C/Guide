---
title: "Installing VitaShell"
---

Our next step is to install VitaShell, which is similar to molecularShell but has more features.
{: .notice--info}

#### What you need

* The latest release of [VitaShell](https://github.com/TheOfficialFloW/VitaShell/releases/latest)
* An FTP Client such as [FileZilla](https://filezilla-project.org/download.php)
* An internet connection on your PS Vita and your PC

#### Instructions

1. Open molecularShell on your PS Vita
1. Press Select to start the built-in FTP Server
1. On your PC, connect to your PS Vita using your FTP client
1. Copy the VitaShell `.vpk` to `ux0:` folder

    ![]({{ base_path }}/images/screenshots/filezilla-vitashell.png)
    {: .notice--info}

1. Press ⭕ on your PS Vita to close the FTP Server
1. Navigate to the `ux0:` folder on your PS Vita
1. Select the VitaShell `.vpk` and press X
1. Press X to dismiss the warning and VitaShell will install
1. Your device should now have VitaShell installed

    ![]({{ base_path }}/images/screenshots/vitashell.png)
    {: .notice--info}

Now that VitaShell is installed you can transfer files to PS Vita systems via a USB cable or via a flash drive for PS TV systems.
{: .notice--info}
