Our next step is to install VitaShell
{: .notice--info}

Soundhax is compatible with versions 9.0.0 through 11.3.0 in the EUR, JPN, KOR, and USA regions.
{: .notice--info}


<div class="notice--warning">{{ notice-1 | markdownify }}</div>

#### What you need

* The latest release of [VitaShell](https://github.com/TheOfficialFloW/VitaShell/releases/latest)
* An FTP Client such as [FileZilla](https://filezilla-project.org/download.php)
* An internet connection on your Vita and your PC

#### Instructions

1. Open molecularShell on your Vita
1. Press Select to start the built-in FTP Server
1. On your PC, connect to your Vita using your FTP client
1. Copy the VitaShell `.vpk` to `ux0:` folder

    ![]({{ base_path }}/images/screenshots/soundhax-file-layout.png)
    {: .notice--info}

1. Press â­• on your Vita to close the FTP Server
1. Navigate to the `ux0:` folder on your Vita
1. Select the VitaShell `.vpk` and press X
1. Press X to dismiss the warning and VitaShell will install
1. Your device should now have VitaShell installed

    ![]({{ base_path }}/images/screenshots/vitashell.png)
    {: .notice--info}
